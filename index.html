How to Play

  • Guess the 4-letter word in 6 tries
  • Each guess must be a valid 4-letter word with unique letters
  • After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was
  • Green circles show correct letters in correct position
  • Yellow circles show correct letters in wrong position
